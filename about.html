About Us

Wholesale textile and industrial supply partner

Company Overview

Pehlari Trading Pvt Ltd is a wholesale supplier of textile accessories and industrial utility products based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. We cater to retailers, bulk buyers, institutions, and government procurement requirements.

Our product range includes hook & loop tapes, threads, tailoring accessories, adhesives, nets, and other supporting materials used across textile, garment, furnishing, and industrial applications.

With a focus on consistent quality and dependable supply, we work closely with buyers to meet bulk quantity requirements and application-specific needs.

Markets We Serve

  • Retailers and distributors
  • Institutions and uniform suppliers
  • Government and tender-based procurement
